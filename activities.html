Kigali is far more than just a transit hub. The city buzzes with life from sunrise to sunset — offering visitors an incredible variety of experiences that blend history, innovation, art, outdoor adventure, and vibrant nightlife.

Whether you're a history buff, an adrenaline junkie, a wellness seeker, or simply a curious traveler, Kigali has something extraordinary waiting for you.

Must-Do Experiences

Hand-picked activities every visitor to Kigali should experience

History

Kigali Genocide Memorial

Gisozi, Kigali

The Kigali Genocide Memorial is the final resting place for more than 250,000 victims of the 1994 genocide against the Tutsi. The museum presents a deeply moving and educational account of the genocide, Rwanda's recovery, and the importance of remembrance. A visit here is both sobering and inspiring.

2–3 hours Free entry Mon–Sun: 8am–5pm

Arts

Inema Arts Center

Kacyiru, Kigali

Founded by brothers Emmanuel and Innocent Nkurunziza, Inema Arts Center is a vibrant hub for contemporary Rwandan art. Visit the gallery to see incredible paintings, attend a live music or dance performance, or take an art class. The center represents the best of Kigali's booming creative scene.

1–2 hours Small entry fee Daily: 9am–6pm

Adventure

Kigali Cable Car (Gondola)

Nyarugenge, Kigali

Soar above the rooftops and rolling hills of Kigali in the city's iconic gondola cable car. The ride offers spectacular 360° views of the city skyline and surrounding valleys. Perfect for photographers, couples, and anyone seeking a unique perspective on Africa's cleanest capital.

30 min ride ~$5 per trip Daily: 7am–7pm

Adventure

Mountain Biking Tours

Various Kigali Hills

Rwanda is known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills" — and exploring them by bike is an unforgettable experience. Local operators offer guided mountain biking tours through the city's neighborhoods, rural villages, and stunning hillside tea plantations. Suitable for all fitness levels.

Half or full day From ~$30 Bookable daily

Culture

Nyamirambo Walking Tour

Nyamirambo, Kigali

Take a guided walking tour through Nyamirambo — Kigali's oldest and most vibrant neighborhood. Known for its colorful market stalls, street food, mosques, churches, and welcoming locals, this neighborhood gives you a genuine taste of everyday Kigali life. Tours are run by Nyamirambo Women's Center.

3–4 hours ~$20 guided tour By appointment
Book Tour ↗
Leisure

Kigali Golf Club

Nyamirambo, Kigali

One of the oldest golf clubs in Central Africa, the Kigali Golf Club features an 18-hole course set among the rolling green hills. Visitors can rent equipment, take lessons, or simply enjoy the stunning views. The clubhouse is also a great spot for sundowners after a game.

4–5 hours ~$30–50/round Daily: 6am–6pm

Top 5 Free Activities

  1. Kigali Genocide Memorial — Free entry, mandatory experience
  2. Explore Nyamirambo — Walk the streets of Kigali's historic quarter
  3. Visit Kimironko Market — Window-shop Rwanda's largest market
  4. Kigali Public Library — Beautiful architecture and local culture
  5. Walk the Kigali Convention Centre — Admire the iconic dome architecture
