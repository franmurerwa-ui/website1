About the City

Why Kigali is Africa's
Most Exciting Destination

Kigali, the capital of Rwanda, is one of Africa's most vibrant, safe, and rapidly growing cities. Nestled among rolling green hills, this city offers a unique blend of modern infrastructure and authentic African culture.

Recognized globally for its exceptional cleanliness and order, Kigali has transformed into a top-tier destination for travelers seeking adventure, gastronomy, culture, and nature — all in one place.

  • Ranked Africa's Cleanest City multiple years running
  • Surrounded by 1,000 hills and stunning natural scenery
  • ️ Booming food scene with local and international cuisine
  • Rich arts & cultural heritage rooted in Rwandan tradition
  • ️ World-class Kigali International Airport hub

Top 5 Must-Do Experiences

In order of visitor recommendation

  1. Kigali Genocide Memorial A deeply moving tribute to the 1994 genocide — essential for understanding Rwanda's history
  2. Gorilla Trekking via Volcanoes NP A once-in-a-lifetime encounter with mountain gorillas in their natural habitat
  3. Nyamirambo Walking Tour Discover Kigali's vibrant oldest neighborhood with local guides and street food
  4. Inema Arts Center Meet contemporary Rwandan artists and experience live performances
  5. Kimironko Market Shop for fresh produce, handcrafted goods, and colorful Ankara fabrics

️ Traveler Tips for Kigali

Things every visitor should know

  • Visitors from most countries can get a visa on arrival or e-visa online
  • Plastic bags are banned — bring reusable alternatives
  • Last Saturday of each month is Umuganda (community cleaning day)
  • Yellow fever vaccination certificate required for entry
  • MTN and Airtel offer affordable SIM cards with data
  • ️ Always agree on a moto-taxi price before boarding
  • ️ Sunscreen is essential — the equatorial sun is intense
  • Try Primus or Mutzig — Rwanda's local beer brands
